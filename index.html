About

Hi, I'm Eunice Abena Lettu, a passionate and detail-oriented software engineer with a strong foundation in web development, problem-solving, and clean code principles.
With hands-on experience in JavaScript, TypeScript, React, and Python, I enjoy crafting responsive and accessible user interfaces, optimizing performance, and collaborating on scalable solutions that make a real-world impact. I believe great software is built at the intersection of clarity, creativity, and communication.
Over the past few years, I've contributed to diverse projects—ranging from e-commerce platforms and academic tools to open-source applications—always with a focus on usability, resilience, and maintainability.

My Mission

To build technology that is inclusive, efficient, and thoughtfully designed—while continuously improving as both a developer and a human.
When I'm not coding, you'll find me mentoring others, learning about accessibility, or exploring creative hobbies like design and photography.

Want to work together or just say hi? Contact Me — I'm always open to meaningful collaborations and exciting challenges!
